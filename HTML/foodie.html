Blackened chicken recipe.

  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts.
  • Italian seasoning herbs.
  • Spices: Paprika, cumin, cayenne pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, kosher salt and ground black pepper.
  • Canola oil.

How to make blackened chicken from scratch.

  1. Whisk: First, whisk the seasonings together in a shallow dish or small bowl.
  2. Season: Next, pat dry with a paper towel before coating with oil and tossing it in the seasoning. Press the blackening spice so it sticks to the side of the chicken.
  3. Sear: Then, on a hot skillet over high heat, cook on each side for about 30 seconds to sear it.
  4. Bake: Last, transfer the skillet to the oven and cook for 12 to 15 minutes at 400 degrees F.
  5. Serve: Finally, place chicken on a platter and cover with foil. Let it rest for five minutes before serving.
